Our Story

From the Heart
of Cameroun

Golden Cocoa was born from a passion for authenticity. We source only the finest cacao beans from the rich volcanic soils of Cameroun — where cacao has been cultivated for generations with artisanal care and deep respect for the land.

Our cocoa powder undergoes a natural drying and grinding process with absolutely no additives, no sugar, and no preservatives. What you receive is pure, raw cacao — exactly as nature intended.

Based in Rose Hill, Beau Bassin, we are proud to bring premium African cacao directly to the families, bakeries, and chocolate lovers of Mauritius.
